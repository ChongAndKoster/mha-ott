Payment Instructions

You are eligible to receive up to $60 total in the study: $40 through the completion of surveys, and $20 for consistent engagement with the digital mental health tool throughout the first four weeks of the study.

Your payment will be split into 6 installments. With each installment you will be eligible to earn $10. For the first 4 weeks, half of your weekly incentive ($5) will be based on engagement with the digital health tool, and the other half ($5) will be based on the completion of the weekly surveys.

More specifically, $5 will be designated if you engage with the tool 3 times or more each week. If you engage the tool less than 3 times in a week, you will not be compensated for this component for that week. Compensation to each part is independent of one another, so you can earn $5 for survey completion even if you haven’t engaged sufficiently to earn $5 for resource engagement. During week 5, you will be sent an exit survey for which you will receive $10 if completed. Completion of the follow up survey that will be sent to you at the end of week 8 will also be tied to a $10 incentive amount.

If you complete all six surveys and engage at least 3x weekly with the DIY tool during the first 4 weeks, you will receive $60 in Amazon gift codes. Gift codes for each week’s resource engagement and survey completion will be emailed to you within 7-10 business days of each week’s survey completion period. Payment is sent to participants who provide complete and consistently accurate data. If we find later that you have not engaged to this standard, we will let you know and your participation in the study will end. If you do not receive payment, please contact the study team at mentalhealthstudy@uw.edu
